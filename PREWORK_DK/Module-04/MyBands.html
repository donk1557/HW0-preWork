Koji Kondo- The Legend, The Myth, The Mario

Born August 1961, Koji Kondo began learning piano from the age of 5.
Kondo attended Osaka University of Arts; where he gained some experience composing and arranging pieces using a piano and a computer for programming sounds into BASIC.
In 1984, Kondo was hired by Nintendo and would later go on to produce some of the most memorable scores for the best videogame franchises like Super Mario World, Legend of Zelda, and Star Fox.
The early 2000s was the last time Kondo would write all composition by himself; nowadays, Kondo supervises and collaborates with other staff members on musical compositions for games.
